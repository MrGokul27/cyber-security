Our Story

In 1998, Stackly began as a small boutique security firm specializing in cryptographic protocols for financial institutions. Our founders realized that as the digital landscape expanded, perimeter defense alone would eventually fail.

By 2010, we pioneered the first behavioral-based threat detection engine, moving away from static signatures. Today, we stand as a global leader in autonomous threat intelligence, managing security for the world's most critical infrastructure with mathematical precision.
